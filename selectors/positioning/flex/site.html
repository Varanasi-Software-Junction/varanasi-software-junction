THE STAR HOTELL

Patterned blanket on wooden bed in grey bedroom interior with plants and posters.

Light plays a major role in any space, as it helps in creating a required ambiance. Make sure to

add both the direct and indirect lighting.

Add table lamps on either side of the bed with a reading light too, that can be separately controlled. Make sure to add focus lights for showcasing any decorative accent, art pieces. Even adding task light to closets, that would turn on automatically when the closet door is opened would be a great addition too! Moreover, make sure to provide ample lighting around the makeup mirror vanity. Additionally dimmer to controls on!
